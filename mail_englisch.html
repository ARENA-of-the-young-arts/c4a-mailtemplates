We are looking for you!

ARENA… of the young arts is a festival for dance, theatre and performance and was established in 1991 in Erlangen, Germany. The festival considers itself to be a platform for interdisciplinary, contemporary and experimental productions. A special focus lies on projects that take place in public spaces or less conventional theatre spaces. In 2023 the 33rd ARENA festival is themed ›CHAOS‹ and is going to take place from June 21st-25th. Artists from all over the world are invited to deal with the theme individually and creatively.

This year we are once again looking forward to receiving diverse applications from the fields of theatre, dance and performance. These can be submitted via the application form on our website arena-festival.de until the 31st of December 2022.

As the festival is organised by students of the University Erlangen-Nuremberg voluntarily, we can only cover the artists’ travel expenses, accommodation and catering but no further payment.

We would like to ask you to publish this information as well as the enclosed call for applications in order to give young artists the opportunity to apply to us. If you are interested or have any questions, please feel free to contact us at any time.

Motto Text 2023

The world is in shambles. Confusion surrounds us. An utter mess - complete lack of order. An overwhelming vacuum wraps around us as we sink into overcrowded blank spaces. We wander around, confuse ourselves, lose ourselves. We spin in circles, emptying our thoughts until we drown in an everlasting vastness. We manoeuvre through a labyrinth that buries us under messages, information and impressions. It is no longer possible to know everything. It is no longer possible to follow everything, invest the same energy in everything, to get involved, to stand up, to rebuild. Everything we build crumbles, everything we create collapses, everything that encircles us causes us to tumble.

Hazily in a cloud of ash, we wander around, running seemingly aimlessly into walls, until we ourselves turn into chaos. What remains is to embrace the change and to continuously find sense in nonsense. In knowing that nothing is infinite, we harbour immensely creative potential. We aim to explore how we can artistically counter the chaos. We sample spectacular entropies and reclaim the space that is ours. We break out of the mess and turn the disarray into something constructive.

Let’s take the chaos into our hands.
Let’s be alive once again.
Let’s dance in the hullabaloo.
Let’s swarm and scurry.
Let’s be chaotic.
